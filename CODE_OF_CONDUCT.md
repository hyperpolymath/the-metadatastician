# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to an open, respectful, and safe collaboration environment for all contributors.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus critique on ideas, not people.
- Use clear, evidence-based technical discussion.
- Respect consent, authorship, and attribution boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of others' work.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to: `jonathan@metadatastician.art`

Reports will be handled promptly and with discretion.
